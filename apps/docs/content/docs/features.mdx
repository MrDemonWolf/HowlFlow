# Features

## Schedule Blocks

HowlFlow divides your day into 12 wolf-themed time blocks, generated automatically from your wake-up time:

- **Morning Howl** — Wake up and get moving
- **Dawn Hunt** — First focused work session
- **Pack Patrol** — Mid-morning tasks
- And more through to **Moonlight Wind Down**

Each block contains subtasks you can check off. The schedule adapts to your chosen wake time and adjusts block durations accordingly.

## Focus Timer

A Pomodoro-style timer with configurable work and break durations:

- Visual progress ring showing time remaining
- Haptic feedback on completion
- Background notifications when the timer ends
- Work/break cycle tracking

The timer runs via `setInterval` in the screen component. When the app is backgrounded, a local notification is scheduled for the remaining time.

## Brain Dump

Quick-capture for thoughts before they disappear:

- Tap to add a new thought instantly
- No folders, no categories — just a running list
- Swipe to delete when processed
- Persisted locally via MMKV

## XP & Level System

Gamification to keep you motivated:

- Earn XP for completing subtasks and focus sessions
- Wolf rank progression from **Pup** to **Lone Wolf Legend**
- Daily streak tracking with **grace days** — miss a day without losing your streak
- Stats dashboard showing your progress over time
